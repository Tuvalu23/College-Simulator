Dear {{ name }},

Congratulations! It is an honor to invite you to join Carnegie Mellon University’s College of Computer Science for Fall 2025. If you enroll in the College of Computer Science, you will have the opportunity to select a major from the departments of Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Human-Computer Interaction, Robotics or Computational Biology, as well as our double major programs.

Each year, Carnegie Mellon receives an increasing number of applications from very talented students. After reviewing the academic and supporting credentials of all of our applicants, we believe that you can be very successful at Carnegie Mellon.

On behalf of the university, I want to express our confidence in you. We know that you will greatly contribute to our campus community, both in academics and extracurricular activities. As a university, we are committed to meeting your need for intellectual and personal growth, and we hope you will make Carnegie Mellon your university.

To accept our offer of admission, please submit an $800 deposit by VISA or MasterCard at https://s3.andrew.cmu.edu/aio on or before February 15, 2025. This deposit will be credited to your account and is not refundable or transferable. Because you have been accepted to Carnegie Mellon under the early decision plan, you should make plans to withdraw applications from other colleges or universities.

For information relating to financial aid for early decision candidates, please visit https://s3.andrew.cmu.edu/aio, your "Where Am I in the Process" portal. This is where you’ll be able to keep track of your financial aid and admission statuses, missing financial aid documents or any other documentation we may still need from you. You’ll also pay your enrollment deposit through this portal. In the spring, you’ll receive more information on residence hall and dining options. If you have not received this material by April 15, please contact the Office of Admission. More details concerning your course selection, registration, and first-year orientation will be sent in early June.

For the remainder of the year, we know you will maintain the same level of academic performance and community conduct which has resulted in your acceptance to Carnegie Mellon. In the Common Application materials, you will find a Candidate Mid-Year High School Grade Report. Ask your school or college counselor to complete and return this form to our office after mid-year grades are reported in February. We will send a request for final grades and high school graduation confirmation in late May.

Sincerely,

John D. Smith
Senior Admission Officer
