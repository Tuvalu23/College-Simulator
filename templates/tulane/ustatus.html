Green Wave Portal

Welcome to the Tulane application portal for full-time undergraduates!

All applicants must apply via the Common App. Freshman (first-year) students should use this link, while transferring college/university students should use this link. For information on each full-time undergraduate application option and deadline, visit admission.tulane.edu/apply/deadlines-forms.

It typically takes 24-48 hours for Tulane to receive a Common App after it has been submitted. As soon as we receive your application, you will receive an email notification with credentials to log into the Green Wave Portal and review your checklist. Please do not create your own account via this portal.

The Academic, Service, & Leadership Scholarship application, available to freshmen only, must be submitted by December 5. To submit a scholarship application, your Common Application must first arrive to Tulane, then you may click the "Start New Application" link at the bottom of this page to begin.
