Texas Exes Scholarships: The Texas Exes, the university’s official alumni association, administers hundreds of scholarships available to students who enroll at UT Austin. To be considered for a scholarship, you must complete the Texas Exes entering freshman scholarship application, which will automatically place you in consideration for the Texas Exes scholarships for which you qualify, except for the Forty Acres Scholars Program, which will use your admissions application materials for initial consideration. The scholarship application opens on September 1 and closes on March 1.
