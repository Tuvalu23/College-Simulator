Congratulations! The admissions selection committee in the College of Arts and Sciences has approved your application for admission to Cornell University for the fall of 2025. I am honored to share this wonderful news with you and to welcome you to the Cornell community.

As you celebrate your admission to Cornell, I invite you to reflect upon the words and wisdom of Ezra Cornell: "I would found an institution where any person can find instruction in any study." Cornell University gathers people from around the world committed to personal discovery and growth. Our current students, faculty, staff, and generations of alumni aim to encourage your scholarship and creativity and to develop your human spirit alongside future friends in your class. I look forward to having you join the tradition of excellence, ingenuity, diversity, inclusion, and equity that has always made Cornell University distinctive.

We will reserve a place in the class for you when we receive your online response form confirming your intent to enroll and your enrollment deposit. Our offer of admission is contingent upon you satisfactorily completing any schoolwork in progress and on your upholding high standards of character in all activities within and outside of the classroom. Please also note that, upon your enrollment at Cornell, you will be subject to and will be expected to adhere to Cornell University's Code of Academic Integrity. Your next step is to complete the online Enrollment Response Form.

Congratulations again on your admission to Cornell University! I look forward to working with you to ensure that your experiences here will include all of the benefits and opportunities you seek.

With best regards,
