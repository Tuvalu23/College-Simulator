Dear {{ name }}:

Congratulations! We are thrilled to inform you that you have been admitted to UCLA for the Fall Quarter 2025. You have been offered admission to the School of the Arts and Sciences with Computer Science as your academic major.

Being admitted from the waitlist is a testament to your exceptional academic and personal achievements. You were selected from the largest college applicant pool in the nation—nearly 146,000 students applied to UCLA. Your talents and accomplishments have distinguished you, and we are confident that you will thrive here. With over 125 majors and 90+ minors, the academic breadth and research opportunities available to you as a student are second to none. You will also receive personal attention from instructors: nearly two out of three undergraduate classes have fewer than 30 students. Our campus community offers over 1,000 clubs and organizations, providing countless opportunities to engage and explore with your fellow Bruins.

UCLA prepares you to step into your future through our unique combination of cutting-edge academic programs, a diverse and engaging residential experience, support for post-graduate and career exploration, and a college town located in Los Angeles, one of the most innovative and diverse cities in the world. Our world-class faculty and state-of-the-art facilities ensure that you will have everything you need to succeed and make an impact.

As a Bruin, you are joining a community that values inclusivity, innovation, and leadership. Your journey at UCLA will be filled with opportunities to expand your horizons and create lifelong connections. We encourage you to take full advantage of everything UCLA has to offer.

To confirm your intent to enroll, please visit your MyUCLA portal. You will need to submit your Statement of Intent to Register (SIR) by July 1, 2025, along with a non-refundable deposit of $250. Detailed instructions and resources to guide you through the process are available on the portal.

Thank you for your patience throughout the waitlist process. We are delighted to welcome you to the Bruin family. Congratulations once again on this outstanding achievement!

Go Bruins!
