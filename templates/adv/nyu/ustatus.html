Financial Aid Requirements

All required applications must be submitted on time to be considered for a majority of institutional scholarships. Most scholarships at NYU are based on financial need.

  • If you are a domestic applicant, please be sure the SSN on your Common Application matches the SSN provided on your financial aid application(s). To correct your Common Application SSN, please use the "NYU Personal Information Update Request" button.

  • NYU does not participate in IDOC and you should not send copies of taxes or W2 statements unless we specifically request them. If documentation is required, we will send instructions on how to securely submit it to NYU.

We will begin updating checklist statuses in December 2024. Please log directly into your financial aid application and be sure that you have added NYU to your list of schools scheduled to receive it.  Once you have added NYU’s school code, you can be confident that we have received your application.  In December, after we begin updating checklists, it can take up to 7 business days for your checklist to accurately reflect what you have submitted.

Please contact the Office of Financial Aid directly at 212-998-4444 or financial.aid@nyu.edu if you have questions about your financial aid checklist.
