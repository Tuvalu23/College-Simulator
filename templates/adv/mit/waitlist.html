Dear {{ name }},

Thank you for applying to the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. After a careful and thoughtful review of your application, we are unable to offer you admission at this time. However, we are pleased to place you on our waitlist for the Class of 2029.

This year’s applicant pool was one of the most competitive in our history, filled with exceptional students from around the world. Your academic achievements, personal qualities, and commitment to making an impact stood out to us, and we deeply appreciate the time and effort you invested in your application. Being placed on our waitlist reflects our belief in your potential and your fit for MIT.

If you would like to remain on the waitlist, please confirm your interest through your MIT application portal by April 15, 2025. We also encourage you to provide any updates on significant academic or personal accomplishments since your original application, as this information can be valuable during our ongoing review process.

Please note that being on the waitlist does not guarantee admission, as the number of spaces available depends on the decisions of other admitted students. We will notify waitlisted candidates of their final status by late May.

We understand this news may bring a mix of emotions, and we encourage you to focus on the exciting opportunities ahead, regardless of the outcome. Whatever path you choose, we are confident that you will continue to thrive and make meaningful contributions to your community and the world.

Thank you again for applying to MIT. We wish you the best in all your future endeavors and hope to connect with you in the coming months.


Sincerely,
 ​
Stuart Schmill
Dean of Admissions and Student Financial Services
