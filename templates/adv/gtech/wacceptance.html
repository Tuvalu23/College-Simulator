Congratulations! You have been accepted to the Georgia Institute of Technology from our waitlist! We are pleased to offer you admission as a first-year student for the Fall 2025 semester with the major of Computer Science.

We are impressed by your academic achievements and your contributions outside the classroom. Your perseverance and accomplishments stood out to the Admissions Committee, and we are thrilled to welcome you to the Yellow Jacket family!

Georgia Tech offers a unique combination of academic excellence, a dynamic and diverse campus community, and the advantages of being located in Atlanta. We are confident that you will thrive here and make meaningful contributions to our university.

To confirm your place in the incoming class, please visit admitted.gatech.edu and submit your non-refundable $250 admission deposit within seven days from today. If this amount presents a financial burden, please contact our office for assistance.

As a reminder, our offer of admission is contingent upon the continued high level of academic performance and personal character you demonstrated during the application process. We require an official final transcript upon your graduation to confirm your grades and coursework. Georgia Tech reserves the right to rescind this offer if there is a significant decline in academic performance, rigor, or disciplinary conduct.

We look forward to welcoming you to the Georgia Tech family! If you have any questions or need assistance, please do not hesitate to contact our office.
