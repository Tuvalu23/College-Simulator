Upload Materials

The material upload should be used to send updates to your application including items that may be required by the Office of Virginia Status. Please allow a few business days for the document to be reviewed and filed.

We respectfully ask that you refrain from submitting resumes, abstracts, financial aid documents, art supplements, or recommendations through the uploader. Transcripts (other than mid-year grades) and recommendations must be sent directly from your school official.
 

Request to Withdraw Your Application

If you would like to withdraw your application, please complete the Application Withdraw Form. Once you submit the form, the decision is final and you cannot submit another application for this cycle.
