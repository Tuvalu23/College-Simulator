Congratulations,
{{ name }}!

You’re in! We’re thrilled to offer you admission to WashU’s Class of 2029 in the James McKelvey School of Engineering.

The Admissions Committee enjoyed getting to know you and was impressed with your many achievements. We see you at WashU, and we are excited to provide the intellectual and creative support to help you further develop your talents, hone your skills, and explore the things you love. The Class of 2029 will be stronger and brighter with the benefit of your unique perspectives, and we can’t wait to see your impact on our campus and the world!

As an Early Decision admit, your offer is binding. By accepting this offer, you commit to joining the WashU community and withdrawing any active applications to other colleges and universities. We are excited to welcome you as a part of our dynamic and vibrant class!

It’s time to celebrate! Please take a look at the next steps below, and you’ll be well on your way to becoming an official WashU Bear:

  • Download your official letter of admission via the link above.
  • If you applied for financial assistance, be sure to view your financial assistance offer in the Financial Aid Portal. Visit financialaid.wustl.edu to learn more about financing your education.
  • Submit your Acceptance Form via the WashU Pathway as soon as possible to confirm your binding commitment.
  • Keep an eye out for your Enrollment Preparation Checklist, which will guide you through the next steps to get ready for your first semester at WashU.

We are so excited to welcome you to the WashU community! If you have any questions, don’t hesitate to contact us at admissions@wustl.edu or (800) 638-0700.
